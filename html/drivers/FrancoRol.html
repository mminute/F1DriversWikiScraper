Franco Rol (June 5, 1908 in Turin – June 18, 1977 in Rapallo[1]) was a racing driver from Italy. He participated in five Formula One World Championship Grands Prix, debuting on May 21, 1950. He scored no championship points. He also participated in many non-championship Formula One events.
