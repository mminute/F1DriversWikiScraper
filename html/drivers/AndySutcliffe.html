Andy Sutcliffe (9 May 1947 – 13 July 2015)[1] was a British racing driver from England. After a promising start in Formula 3, his single attempt at Formula One was at the 1977 British Grand Prix with the RAM team, in a March 761. He failed to pre-qualify.[2] A previous entry in the 1974 race, with Scuderia Finotto in a Brabham BT42 resulted in a failure to appear.[3]
