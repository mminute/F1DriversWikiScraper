Robert 'Rob' Schroeder (May 11, 1926 − December 3, 2011) was a racing driver from the United States, born in El Dorado.[1][2][3][4] He participated in one Formula One World Championship Grand Prix, the 1962 United States Grand Prix, on October 7, 1962. He finished tenth, seven laps behind the winner, and scored no championship points.[5] He died after suffering from a short period of ill-health and heart problems in a Dallas hospital in 2011.[2][3][4]
